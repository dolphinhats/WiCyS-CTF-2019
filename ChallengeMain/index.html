Intro

What is secure coding? Well, it is an art of coding and wearing an attacker's hat at the same time. Think strategically:

  • Can someone bypass your credentials checking via something that your program does not expect to see?
  • Can someone add/edit the original request to your app?
  • Does your app know how to validate user's input?
  • Does your app know which commands are valid and which are not?
  • Does your app have a debug mode turned on that helps attackers to receive valuable information?

Code, think (take deliberate time to identify threats and potential points of entry into your system or app), analyze, fix, and then repeat.
